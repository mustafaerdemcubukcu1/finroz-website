Problems We Solve

If this sounds
familiar.

Most of the companies we work with are not in crisis. They are growing — and finding that their financial infrastructure is not keeping pace.

01
Growth without financial visibility
Revenue is growing but cash flow feels unpredictable. You are making decisions without a clear forward view.
02
Working capital under pressure
Collection cycles, supplier terms, and seasonal swings are creating recurring stress — without a structural solution.
03
Banking relationships becoming harder to manage
Credit lines feel reactive rather than strategic. The bank knows your past but not your trajectory.
04
Finance function not scaling with the business
Your team handles transactions well — but strategic financial thinking sits in a gap. A full-time CFO may not yet be the answer.
05
Board or ownership asking harder questions
Investors, family shareholders, or new board members are raising the bar on financial governance and reporting.

About the Founder
"The best financial decisions aren't made with more data — they're made with the right clarity at the right moment."

M. Erdem Çubukçu brings over 15 years of operator-level experience at the intersection of corporate banking, treasury management, and strategic finance.

As former Treasury & Cash Management Director at a leading Turkish conglomerate — overseeing multi-currency liquidity, banking relationships, and financial governance across complex operating structures — he developed the institutional instinct that now defines Finroz Partners.

Earlier career spanning commercial portfolio management and corporate banking business development across Turkey's major financial institutions gave him a 360° view of how capital flows, decisions stall, and growth either accelerates or quietly gets constrained by its own financial architecture.

Finroz was founded on a single conviction: the financial intelligence available to large institutions should be accessible to every serious company — and every serious individual.
